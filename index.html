Gone Phishing

By Jacob Edelin

Phishing occurs when hackers send people messages posing as reputable companies with the intent of deceiving them into revealing sensitive information or downloading malware. Thousands of internet users fall for them every year, would you be one of them?

From: "SunTrust"secure@suntrust.com, To: Me, Subject: Account Temporarily Suspended, Date: 05-12-2023

Dear SunTrust Client,

As part of our security measures, we regularly screen activity in the suntrust Online Banking System. We recently contacted you after noticing on your online account, which is been accessed unusually.

To view your Account,

1. Visit suntrust.com, 2. Sign on to Online Banking with your user ID and password, 3. Select your account

We appreciate your business and are comitted to helping you reach your financial goals. call us at 800-SUNTRUST (786-8789), or stop by your local branch to learn more about our helpful products and services.

Thank you for banking with SunTrust.

Sincerely,

SunTrust Customer Care
